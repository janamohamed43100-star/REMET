# Remet 📌

**Remet** is a digital gateway designed to transform how people experience Egypt. By connecting travelers directly with local experts, we ensure every journey is authentic, secure, and deeply personal.

---

## 🌟 Our Mission
Our platform serves two main purposes:
1. **For International Tourists:** To bridge the gap between visitors and local guides, providing a "local eye" perspective that traditional tourism often misses.
2. **For the Egyptian Community:** To provide a dedicated service for Egyptians to learn more about their own country, history, and hidden gems through curated local trips.

## ✨ Key Features
* **Authentic Exploration:** Discover destinations through the stories and knowledge of those who live there.
* **Personalized Experiences:** Tailored travel plans that match your interests.
* **Safe & Secure:** A trusted platform for finding verified local guides.
* **Cultural Connection:** Promoting deep cultural exchange and supporting local tourism experts.
